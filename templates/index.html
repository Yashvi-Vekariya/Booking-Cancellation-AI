About This Project

This Hotel Reservation Prediction system uses machine learning to forecast booking cancellations based on various factors. By analyzing patterns in guest data, hotels can optimize room allocation and maximize revenue.

The model considers factors such as lead time, pricing, meal preferences, and booking channels to determine the likelihood of cancellation.
